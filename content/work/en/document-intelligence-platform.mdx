## The problem

The client's customer success team was drowning. Every support ticket required analysts to manually search through a knowledge base of 12,000+ documents — compliance guides, product specs, previous tickets. Response time was averaging 6 hours; target was 1.

## Approach

I led the design and implementation of a RAG-powered knowledge agent embedded directly in the support tool.

**Key decisions:**

- **Hybrid search (BM25 + vector)** instead of pure vector. Tested on their real ticket corpus — hybrid beat pure vector by 18 points on recall@5.
- **Structure-aware chunking** that preserved headings, tables, and code blocks. Cut hallucinations in half vs. naive fixed-size chunking.
- **Human-in-the-loop for low-confidence responses** — if retrieval confidence dropped below a threshold, the agent routed the ticket to a human reviewer instead of guessing.
- **Eval harness from day one.** 200 seed questions, run on every prompt change, with automated regression alerts.

## Result

- **40% reduction in average resolution time**
- Handles 10,000+ support queries per day
- 92% customer satisfaction score on AI-handled tickets (vs. 87% baseline for human responses)
- Zero critical hallucination incidents in first 6 months
